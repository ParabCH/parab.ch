Welcome!

 We are the Psychedelic Awareness & Research Association Basel, short PARAB.
 We are a multidisciplinary student organization with a mission to advance knowledge about the therapeutic use of psychedelics.
We inform professionals as well as the public and support the building of a community around evidence-based psychedelic research and therapy.
Doing so, we pave the way for helpful psychedelic experiences in a safe, legal, and accessible manner.
Here you find notifications of future events, past recordings, research updates, and further interesting information.
